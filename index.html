WELCOME TO THE ADMINISTRATION DATABASE

In 1954, following an unprecedented anomalous fracture in reality, the United States and the Soviet Union signed the Secret Geneva Accords. The International Anomalous Border Administration (I.A.B.A.) was established as an independent, neutral agency to contain, study, and keep these non-Euclidean spaces hidden from the public eye.

The space is not structured by traditional linear levels, but rather generated via massive global Biomes. The architecture and artifacts recovered within the anomaly are strictly bound to a temporal anchor, ranging from the years 1895 to 2010. No items outside of this 115-year historical window can exist inside the structure.

Database Architecture:

  • Biomes: Endless regions sharing regional aesthetics (Parisian apartments, Soviet bunkers, Western offices) with chaotic electrical anomalies.
  • Subjects: Biological, physical, or psychological anomalous entities roaming the biomes.
  • Objects: Tools, signboards, architectural elements, and rations processed or manufactured between 1895 and 2010.
